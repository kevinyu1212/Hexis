# HEXIS
Data-driven ecological platform for Hexapoda & Arthropods.

## Architecture
- **Bio-Axis Engine**: Breeding data & Life-cycle tracking
- **AI-Vision Engine**: Species identification & Health scanning
- **Nexus Engine**: Community & Social networking
- **Market/Auction Engine**: Trusted trading & Auction system
- **Guardian Engine**: Security, Auth & Data integrity
- **Command-Axis**: Admin & Governance
